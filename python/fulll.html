RACING RESULTS | WHERE CAN I BET ON THE BETFAIR SP?

Australia – The Results widget will show results for all thoroughbred win markets and all place markets within Australia. All harness win markets and all place markets, except for NSW meetings and any meetings predominantly on Sky 2. Sky 2 meetings are win market only. All greyhound win markets and all place markets, except for Darwin greyhounds, which is win markets only.

New Zealand – The Results widget will also show all thoroughbred win markets in New Zealand. The widget will also show all greyhound win markets.

BETFAIR HUB | RACING RESULTS

Betfair’s racing results page is your one-stop shop for the latest results across all three racing codes. It is the only place to find the Betfair Starting Price (BSP) for each runner, enabling our customers to compare and contrast with the Tote price. The percentage difference between the two prices is one of many reasons why seasoned punters and value hunters choose to bet with Betfair, as the BSP of a runner is usually more favourable than the starting price on the Tote.

Harness racing results, greyhound results and horse racing results can all be accessed via the Betfair Hub. Simply select the date of the race in the calendar tab, then click on either the horse, harness or greyhound icon and choose the venue you wish to see the results for. The racing results page is very easy to access and is updated shortly after each race.

Betfair’s price advantage is one of the main incentives for our customers, and the results page spells out in black and white (and green) why better value is one of our unique selling points. Unlike traditional bookmakers, who have the odds stacked in their favour, Betfair’s peer-to-peer Exchange means you’re betting into a pure market. The final column on the racing results page reflects that, with the percentage price more often than not in Betfair’s favour.

The BSP was first introduced in 2016, and has delivered outstanding value for our customers ever since. The BSP enables back and lay punters to have their bets matched on the Betfair Exchange just before a thoroughbred, harness or greyhound race jumps, giving them the best odds. The BSB versus Best Tote Odds comparisons on the results page is clear evidence that the product provides great value for our loyal customers.

So to find out the latest thoroughbred, harness and greyhound results today and every day, head to the Betfair Hub.
